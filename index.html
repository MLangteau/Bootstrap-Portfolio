About Me

Ms. Langteau is a dynamic full stack programmer. Consulting, she has worked as a senior software engineer and programmer analyst at many different companies, such as Maryland National Bank, AT&T, NASA, Coca-Cola Enterprises East, and Health and Human Services. As much as she likes designing websites, she also enjoys getting to the heart of organizations' data analysis requests.

Ms. Langteau enjoys swimming, reading, and singing, not necessarily at the same time. Since moving to a summy location, she also loves to fish (for a good restaurant), scuba dive (at times for lobster!), and travel.
